# Alphinix
Alphinix is a technology platform offering services in education, technology development, and talent solutions for students, institutions, businesses, and organizations.
## Alphinix Startup Details
**Location:** Pune, Maharashtra, India
## Alphinix Business Model
Alphinix serves five main audience segments. These segments are interconnected across the platform's offerings.
### 1. Students
Engineering, diploma, BCA, MCA, and BSc students.
They get:

- Industrial training
- Internships
- Placement preparation
- Certifications
- Career guidance
- Coding programs
- Webinars
- Skill development
**Goal:** To make students industry-ready and help them get placements.
### 2. Colleges
Engineering colleges and polytechnic institutes.
**Services:**

- Campus training programs
- Placement drive organization
- Pre-placement training
- Faculty Development Programs (FDP)
- NAAC / NBA accreditation support
- College software systems (LMS, ERP)
- AI / IoT / Cloud labs setup
- Curriculum consulting
**Goal:** To give college students better placement opportunities.
### 3. Schools (K-12)
For school management and principals.
**Programs:**

- AI and robotics education
- IoT lab setup
- Coding education
- STEM / ATL lab setup
- Hardware kits (Arduino, Raspberry Pi, drones)
- Teacher training
**Goal:** To introduce future tech education at the school level.
### 4. Businesses & Startups
Startup founders and companies.
**Services:**

- Website development
- Mobile app development
- AI solutions
- Automation systems
- Cloud infrastructure
- Digital marketing
- Technology consulting
**Goal:** To provide businesses with digital technology solutions.
### 5. Companies / HR / Hiring Managers
Companies that want to hire talent.
**Services:**

- Permanent recruitment
- Contract staffing
- Pre-trained intern supply
- Technical assessments
- Recruitment process outsourcing
- Bulk hiring drives
**Goal:** To supply companies with skilled candidates.
